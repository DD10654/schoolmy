Campus!

Library

VKE has a library space of 9,600 Sq. ft. area spread across two floors. The library amasses an enormous collection of books ranging from Spirituality, Literature, Science, Marketing, Philosophy, Architecture and Storybooks for kids and Novels to many more.

Music room

We have fully equipped sound treated rooms for tabla, drums, keyboard and a special section for choir recitals. We have a wide range of instruments from Indian classical like Tanpura, Sitar, Harmonium to western instruments like drums, guitar and keyboard.

Computer Lab

Specially equipped with Apple Server and 33 Apple iMac machines for teachers and students. We have Dell and 2 IBM PC Labs with 25 computers each. We teach C++ Language, Adobe Photoshop, Corel draw mobile apps and games.
